Check-in

A is at a check-in desk...

A: Excuse me, can I check in Now?

B: Of course. Would you please give me your passport and ticket?

A: Sure, here you are.

B: Do you have any luggage?

A: No, only my briefcase.

B: Would you prefer a window seat or an aisle seat?

A: A window seat, please.

B: Here are your boarding card, ticket and passport. Your seat number is 12B and the boarding time will be 16:30 at Gate 35.

A: Thanks a lot.

Vocabulary:

Check in 办理登机手续或旅馆入住手续

To register at a hotel or before taking a plane

Passport 护照

A document which demonstrates identity and nationality

Luggage 行李

The cases, bags, boxes, etc., of a traveler.

Briefcase 公文包

A small bag used by business people to carry documents and small items.

Aisle 通道

A narrow passage between rows of seats on a plane.

Boarding card 登机牌

An official card to be given when entering an aircraft.
