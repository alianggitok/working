Immigration control

A is going through the immigration check at the airport...

A: May I see your passport and landing card, please?

B: What's a landing card?

A: This form. Can you fill it in please? So, where do you come from?

B: From China.

A: What's the purpose of your visit?

B: I'm going to study for a Master's degree here.

A: Where will you be staying?

B: In a dormitory on campus.

A: Anything to declare?

B: No, nothing.

A: Good luck with your study.

Vocabulary:

Landing card 入境登记卡

An official card to be given when going through immigration.

Fill in (a form) 填表格

To put in whatever is needed to complete something.

Purpose 目的

Reason for an action.

Dormitory 宿舍

A residence hall for students.

Campus 校园

The grounds and buildings of a university or college.

Declare 申报

To make known publicly or officially, according to rules, custom, etc.
